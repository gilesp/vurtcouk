How To Make Ginger Beer

I fancied a nice cool drink the other night, but I’m fed up with cola, lemonade and juices. I prefer a “drier” soft drink, and one that doesn’t make you want to scrub your teeth immediately after drinking. So I decided to make myself some ginger beer. “Hang on, why didn’t you just buy some ginger beer?” I imagine you asking me. Well, most commercially produced ginger beer is far too sweet and not gingery enough for my taste, that is why.
